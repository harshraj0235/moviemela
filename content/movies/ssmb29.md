# SSMB29 Release Date Confirmed: Mahesh Babu, Cast, Trailer, Story & Budget

*SSMB29* (reportedly titled *Varanasi*) is undoubtedly the most colossal undertaking in the history of Indian cinema. Whenever the master storyteller S.S. Rajamouli announces a project, the world watches. Following the global phenomenon of *RRR*, Rajamouli has joined forces with Telugu superstar Mahesh Babu for an epic, globe-trotting jungle adventure that promises to push the boundaries of Indian filmmaking.

Here is absolutely everything confirmed so far about the *SSMB29* release date, cast, trailer, story, budget, and OTT release details.

## Quick Summary

Before we dive deep into the specific details, here is a quick overview of what you need to know about *SSMB29*:

- **Movie Name:** SSMB29 (Rumored Title: Varanasi)
- **Lead Actor:** Mahesh Babu
- **Supporting Cast:** Priyanka Chopra Jonas, Prithviraj Sukumaran
- **Director:** S.S. Rajamouli

- **Expected Release Date:** April 7, 2027

## Release Date

When will *SSMB29* release in theaters? Given S.S. Rajamouli's notorious perfectionism and the immense scale of the project, the pre-production phase alone took over two years. With filming currently underway across multiple international locations, the post-production and extensive CGI work will take a considerable amount of time.

Industry sources confirm that the makers have locked **April 7, 2027** for a massive global theatrical release. This date strategically aligns with the summer holiday season in India, ensuring record-breaking opening numbers. The film will be released in multiple Indian languages, as well as English, Spanish, and Japanese, marking it as a true global release.

## Cast

Rajamouli is known for casting actors who can dedicate years of their lives to his vision. Here is the confirmed and expected cast list for *SSMB29*:

- **Mahesh Babu:** The superstar plays the lead role of an intrepid explorer. Mahesh Babu has undergone a drastic physical transformation, sporting a rugged look, long hair, and a heavy beard—a stark contrast to his usual polished onscreen persona. Rumors suggest his character traits are deeply inspired by Lord Hanuman.
- **Priyanka Chopra Jonas:** Making her grand return to Telugu cinema, the global icon is reported to be playing a character named Mandakini, a fierce and crucial figure in the narrative.

## Story

The story of *SSMB29* is a sprawling, globe-trotting action-adventure heavily inspired by the works of Wilbur Smith and the *Indiana Jones* franchise, but deeply rooted in Indian mythology. Written by Rajamouli's father, the legendary V. Vijayendra Prasad, the narrative spans centuries, charting events from 512 CE all the way to the modern era.

The film revolves around an ancient, lost civilization and a hidden treasure of unimaginable power. Mahesh Babu's character embarks on a perilous journey across unforgiving terrains—from dense, uncharted jungles of the Amazon to the dry savannas of Africa. Expect high-octane survival sequences, encounters with ferocious wildlife, and massive ancient temple sets. It is a story of discovery, survival, and the ultimate battle between greed and duty.

## Director

**S.S. Rajamouli** needs no introduction. He is the architect behind the *Baahubali* franchise and *RRR*, films that redefined Indian cinema's global reach. With *SSMB29*, Rajamouli is stepping out of period dramas and historical fiction into the realm of pure Indiana Jones-style adventure. His unparalleled ability to execute grand visual spectacles coupled with profound emotional cores guarantees that *SSMB29* will be an immersive cinematic experience.

## Producer

The film is produced by **K.L. Narayana** under the banner of **Sri Durga Arts**. This collaboration has been in the making for over a decade. The production house has given Rajamouli a virtual blank check to ensure the film competes with massive Hollywood tentpoles in terms of visual fidelity and scale.

## Budget

Hold onto your seats. *SSMB29* is officially the most expensive film ever produced in the history of Indian and Asian cinema. The estimated budget is a staggering **₹1,400 Crores** (approximately $170 Million USD). 

This astronomical budget is primarily allocated to the heavy CGI and VFX work, which is being handled by top-tier international studios that have worked on films like *Avatar* and *The Avengers*. Additionally, shooting in extreme, remote international locations and the massive remunerations for the pan-global cast heavily contribute to the cost.

## Filming Location

To capture the true essence of a global adventure, the film is being shot in some of the most exotic and dangerous locations on the planet. Key schedules have taken place in the dense jungles of the **Amazon rainforest**, the deserts of **Namibia**, and various undisclosed locations in **Eastern Europe**. Furthermore, massive, highly detailed sets representing ancient temples and lost cities have been erected at the Ramoji Film City in Hyderabad.

## Music

The Oscar-winning composer **M.M. Keeravani** returns to collaborate with his brother Rajamouli. Given the film's global adventure theme, Keeravani is reportedly blending intense tribal rhythms, grand orchestral symphonies, and traditional Indian classical elements. The background score is expected to be a massive driving force behind the film's thrilling set pieces.

## Runtime

S.S. Rajamouli films are grand epics that require time to unfold. Like *RRR* and *Baahubali*, expect *SSMB29* to comfortably cross the three-hour mark. A runtime of around **3 hours and 10 minutes** is highly likely, allowing the narrative to breathe while delivering non-stop adventure.
